Your Emergency Plan(sub heading) Write out your early warning signs Most mental crises do not pop up suddenly and without warning. On the contrary, there are usually early warning signs that could be helpful to pay attention to. These signs are different from person to person but might include any from this list or others you already know about yourself. See which might apply to you: *tiredness, exhaustion *pain, tightness in the chest, heavy muscles *listlessness, low-to-no motivation *a need to be alone/ withdrawal from friends and family *rumination (loopin9. persistent thoughts) *concentration or memory problems *difficulty making decisions *sleep problems *appetite changes *restlessness, impatience, irritability *sensitivity to noise or other interference *persistent fear or sadness thoughts of death
